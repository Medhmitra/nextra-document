## ** Refund a Sale**

 In the refund panel:
    - Select specific **items** to refund.
    - Choose **Refund Method**: Cash, Card, Other.
    - Assign **Staff** issuing the refund.
    - Add **Refund Reason**: e.g., Overcharge, Unsatisfactory service, Duplicate booking.

### Purpose: Refund a paid sale fully or partially if the customer is unsatisfied or overcharged.

### Steps:

❶ Go to **Sales**, click on a **Sale ID** to open its detail panel.  
❷ Click the **three-dot menu (⋮)** in the top-right → Select **"Refund Sale"**.  
   - Refund option appears **only for Paid Sales**.  
❸ Click **Issue Refund**. Confirmation message will appear.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
This is especially helpful when you're reviewing **past performance**, tracking **busy periods**, or checking **future availability**.
