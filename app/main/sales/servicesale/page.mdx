## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Navigate to Sales**  
On the **main dashboard**, look to the **left sidebar menu** and click on **“Sales”**.

❷ **Go to Services Tab**  
Inside the Sales section, at the **top or tab menu**, you’ll see a navigation option labeled **“Services”** – click on it to enter the **Service Sales** view.

❸ **Browse Service Sales**  
On this page, you’ll find a list of **all sales related to services** like haircuts, facials, or spa treatments.

❹ **Add a New Service Sale**  
Click on the **“Add Sale”** button usually at the **top-right corner** of the page.  
In the opened panel, choose customer type, venue, and select the service(s) from the list to add to the cart.  
You can then proceed to apply discounts, assign staff, and complete payment via your preferred method.

❺ **Edit Existing Sale**  
Click on the **Sale ID** or open the **three-dot (⋮) menu** at the end of the sale row and choose **“Edit Sale”**.

❻ **Issue a Refund**  
Click on the Sale ID → open the three-dot menu → select **“Refund”**, and proceed through the refund process.

❼ **Void a Sale**  
Follow the same path and select **“Void Sale”** from the menu.

❽ **Additional Options**  
You can also **share the invoice**, and **view both sales and client details** directly from this tab.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Service Sales lets you track treatments and staff performance easily, and handle any after-sale corrections efficiently.
