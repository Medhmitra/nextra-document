## Steps:



‚ù∂ **View Sale Details**  
Click on **Sale ID**.  
The detail panel shows:
- Services/products sold  
- Membership or voucher details  
- Tax, discount, payment, and activity log  

‚ù∑ **View Client Details**  
Click on **Client Name**.  
Panel shows:
- Contact info  
- Purchase history  
- Memberships, vouchers, and usage history
