## **Case 10 : Sales – Products**

### Steps to View and Manage Product Sales:

❶ On the **dashboard**, click on **“Sales”**.  
❷ In the top tabs, click **“Products”** to view all **product sales** (e.g., shampoos, conditioners, beauty tools).  
❸ Click **“Add Sale”** to start a new sale involving products.  
❹ Select the **product**, quantity, client, and payment method.  
❺ To **edit a product sale**, click into the Sale ID or three-dot menu and choose **“Edit Sale”**.  
❻ Use the same menu to **refund** or **void** a product sale.  
❼ You can **view invoice**, **client details**, and track **product quantities** from this screen.
