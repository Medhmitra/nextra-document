## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Go to the Sales Section**  
From the **main dashboard**, click on **“Sales”** in the sidebar.

❷ **Open the Packages Tab**  
In the Sales section, locate the **top navigation tab** labeled **“Packages”** and click on it.

❸ **View All Package Sales**  
This section shows all the sales made for **service packages** (e.g., bridal packages, facial bundles, etc.).

❹ **Add a New Package Sale**  
Click on **“Add Sale”** to initiate a new package sale.

❺ **Fill Sale Details**  
Select the **venue**, add the **package**, assign a **client and staff**, and complete the sale using your preferred **payment method**.

❻ **Edit Existing Sale**  
To **edit an existing sale**, click the Sale ID or use the three-dot menu and select **“Edit Sale”**.

❼ **Refund or Void a Sale**  
To **refund** or **void** a package sale, use the Sale ID or three-dot menu options.

❽ **Share and View Details**  
Easily **share invoices**, and view detailed sale or client information from the same tab.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Tracking package sales regularly helps in analyzing client demand, seasonal package success, and revenue optimization.
