## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Go to Sales**  
On the dashboard, click on **“Sales”** in the left-side menu.

❷ **Open Gift Cards Tab**  
Click on the **“Gift Cards”** tab at the top of the Sales page.

❸ **View Existing Sales**  
All **purchased gift cards** and their current usage status are listed here.

❹ **Add a New Sale**  
Click **“Add Sale”** to issue a new gift card to a customer.

❺ **Enter Sale Details**  
Select the **gift card amount**, client, and venue. Complete the sale using a valid payment method.

❻ **Edit or Refund Sales**  
To make changes, click the Sale ID or open the action menu and choose **Edit**, **Refund**, or **Void Sale**.

❼ **Track Gift Card Usage**  
Monitor how much value has been used and check **expiry dates** directly on this screen.

❽ **View Full Client Info**  
Access full **client sales details** and share invoices if needed.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Gift cards are a great way to **boost upfront revenue** and bring back repeat clients.
