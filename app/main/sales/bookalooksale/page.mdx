## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Begin at the Dashboard**  
Click on **“Sales”** in the left menu.

❷ **Select Book A Look**  
In the Sales tab section, choose **“Book A Look”**.

❸ **View Sales List**  
This page lists all the **visual or consultation-based bookings**.

❹ **Add a New Sale**  
Click on **“Add Sale”**, choose **Book A Look** from the available options, fill out required details, and proceed.

❺ **Edit, Refund or Void a Sale**  
Use the **three-dot menu** on the sale row or click into the **Sale ID**.

❻ **Access Client and Sale History**  
You can also **share invoices**, and **view client/sale history** from this panel.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
This is especially helpful when you're reviewing **past performance**, tracking **busy periods**, or checking **future availability**.
