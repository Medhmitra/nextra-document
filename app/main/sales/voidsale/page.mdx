## Void a Sale



### Purpose: Remove an incorrect sale permanently from reports. Cannot be undone.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **From Sales**, click on a **Sale ID**

‚ù∑ **Click three-dot menu (‚ãÆ)** ‚Üí **"Void Sale"**

‚ù∏ A warning message appears:  
- Confirms deletion of sale and payment.

‚ùπ **Click Void Sale to proceed**

Now marked as **Voided** in the sales list.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Use this only for completely incorrect transactions. Once voided, the sale is removed from all reports and can't be recovered.
