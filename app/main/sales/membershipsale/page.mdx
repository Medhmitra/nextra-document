## Membership Sales

 

*Purpose*: View and manage all membership purchases, whether active or expired.

## Steps:



❶ **Navigate to Sales**  
From the main sidebar, click on **Sales**.

❷ **Select the “Memberships” tab**  
This section displays both active and expired **membership purchases**.

❸ **Add a Membership Sale**  
Click **“Add Sale”**, choose a membership plan, and assign it to a client.

❹ **Edit, Refund, or Void Memberships**  
Use the three-dot menu or Sale ID on each entry to perform these actions.

❺ **Review Expiry Dates and Benefits**  
Each entry shows expiry date, benefits, and usage history.

❻ **Access Invoices and Client History**  
Related invoices and full client usage can be accessed directly.

---

**Tip for Owners**:  
Use this section regularly to track client activity and ensure memberships are being utilized.
