## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **In Sales, click on the Sale ID**  
Open the **Sales** section from the main menu and select the **Sale ID** of the invoice you wish to share.

‚ù∑ **Click the "Share Invoice" button**  
At the top of the detail panel, click the **"Share Invoice"** button.

‚ù∏ **Choose sharing method**  
Select from the following options:
- **Email**
- **PDF download**
- **Copy invoice link**

‚ùπ **Invoice details included**  
The shared invoice includes:
- Client information
- Services
- Pricing
- Discounts
- Taxes
- Payment method

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
This feature helps streamline client communication and maintain accurate records of each transaction.
