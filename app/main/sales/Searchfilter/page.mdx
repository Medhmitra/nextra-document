## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **Go to the Sidebar**  
Click on **Sales** in the left-side navigation menu.

‚ù∑ **Locate the Search and Filter Controls**  
At the **top-left** of the Sales page, you‚Äôll find:

- **Search Bar**: Type in the **Sale ID** to locate a specific sale.
- **Filter Buttons**:
  - **Status Filter**: Choose from Paid, Unpaid, Partially Paid, Exchanged, Refunded, Voided.
  - **Total Value Filter**: Set a min/max price to narrow by sale value.
  - **Created At Filter**: Pick a specific **date or date range** to find sales within that time.

‚ù∏ **Apply the Filters**  
Click the filter buttons, input your criteria, and the results will update instantly.

‚ùπ **Reset Filters if Needed**  
To remove filters, click the filter again or clear the values you‚Äôve entered.
