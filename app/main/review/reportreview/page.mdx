## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Navigate to the "Review" section from the main sidebar.**  
❷ **Find the review you want to report in the table/list view.**  
❸ **Click the three-dot menu (⋮) on that review row.**  
❹ **From the dropdown, click on “Report Review.”**  
❺ **A pop-up modal will appear prompting you to select or enter a reason for reporting the review.**  
  - Choose from predefined options: **Spam**, **Duplicate**, or **Fake**, or  
  - Manually type a custom reason in the text field provided.  
❻ **After selecting or typing the reason, click the “Report” button.**  
❼ **A confirmation message will appear to notify that the review has been successfully reported.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Reporting inaccurate or harmful reviews helps preserve the integrity of your business.
