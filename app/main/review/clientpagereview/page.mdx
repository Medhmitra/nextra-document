## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Go to the "Review" section**  
Click on **"Review"** from the main sidebar to view all reviews.

❷ **Locate the Client's Review**  
Find the review associated with the client you want to explore.

❸ **Click the Client's Name**  
In the review row, click directly on the **client’s name**.

❹ **View Client Info Panel**  
A right-side panel will open showing detailed info such as:  
- **Client’s basic information**  
- **Activity logs**  
- **Purchased vouchers, memberships, and gift cards**  
- **Product purchase history**

❺ **Close the Panel**  
Click the **cancel (X) icon** at the top-right to close and return to the reviews page.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Great for quickly checking a client’s loyalty, feedback history, or purchases while resolving reviews.
