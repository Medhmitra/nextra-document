## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Go to the "Review" section**  
Click on **"Review"** from the main sidebar to view all reviews.

‚ù∑ **Locate the Client's Review**  
Find the review associated with the client you want to explore.

‚ù∏ **Click the Client's Name**  
In the review row, click directly on the **client‚Äôs name**.

‚ùπ **View Client Info Panel**  
A right-side panel will open showing detailed info such as:  
- **Client‚Äôs basic information**  
- **Activity logs**  
- **Purchased vouchers, memberships, and gift cards**  
- **Product purchase history**

‚ù∫ **Close the Panel**  
Click the **cancel (X) icon** at the top-right to close and return to the reviews page.
