## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **On the main sidebar, click on the "Review" section to navigate to the reviews page.**  
❷ **Locate the review you want to view, then click on the three-dot menu (⋮) in that review’s row.**  
❸ **From the dropdown options, select “View Review.”**  
❹ **A side panel will slide open on the right side of the screen showing the complete review details:**  
- **Appointment ID**  
- **Date Created**  
- **Star Rating**  
- **Customer Comment**  
- **Reply** (if a reply was previously submitted)  
❺ **If there is no reply submitted yet, an input field will be available to write a response.**  
❻ **Type your response in the field provided.**  
❼ **Once your reply is entered, click the “Save Changes” button at the bottom of the panel to save your response.**  
❽ **A confirmation message will appear indicating the reply has been saved successfully.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Responding promptly shows clients that their feedback is valued and helps improve your salon's credibility.
