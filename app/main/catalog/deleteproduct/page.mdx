## Delete a Product

 

*Purpose*: Safely remove products that are outdated, duplicate, or no longer available.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Locate the Product**  
Find the product in the catalog list. You can use the search bar or scroll through manually.

‚ù∑ **Open the Options Menu**  
Click the **three-dot menu** beside the product, or open the detailed view.

‚ù∏ **Click Delete**  
Choose **Delete** from the menu options.

‚ùπ **Confirm Deletion**  
A confirmation dialog will appear. Confirm your choice to proceed.

‚ù∫ **Success Message**  
Once deleted, the system displays a success message confirming the removal.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Deleted products are permanently removed. Be sure you no longer need the product before confirming deletion.
