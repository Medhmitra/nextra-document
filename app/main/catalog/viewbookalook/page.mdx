## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **From the Book a Look catalog page, click on the Look name**  

❷ **A side panel opens with detailed information**:  
- Shows the **Basic Information**, **Booking Status** (Online, Walk-in), **Assigned Venues**, and **Staff**.

❸ **After reviewing, click the "Cancel/Close" icon to return to the main page**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
This is helpful for reviewing how each Look is configured before editing or duplicating.
