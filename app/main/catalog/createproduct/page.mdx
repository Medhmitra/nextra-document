*Purpose*: Add new items to your catalog including pricing, stock, and categorization.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Go to the Catalog**  
Navigate to the **Catalog** section and click on the **Products** tab.

‚ù∑ **Add a New Product**  
Click the **"Add Product"** button located at the top-right corner.

‚ù∏ **Fill in Product Information**  
The right-side panel opens with a form divided into sections:
- **Basic Info**: Upload Photo, Name, and Description.
- **Categorization**: Choose Product Category, Type, Brand, Gender, Color, Form, Ingredients, and Certificates.
- **Pricing**: Add SKU, Supplier Name, Measurement Unit, Supply Price, Selling Price, Markup, and Tax.
- **Stock**: Assign to Venues with available quantity.

‚ùπ **Save**  
Click **Save** to create the product.

‚ù∫ **Confirmation**  
A success message confirms product creation.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Be detailed with categorization to improve inventory filtering and reporting.
