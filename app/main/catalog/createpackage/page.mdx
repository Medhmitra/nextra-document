## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **Go to the Catalog section and click on the Packages tab**  

‚ù∑ **Click the "Add Package" button at the top-right corner**  

‚ù∏ **A creation panel opens from the side. Enter the Basic Info: Package Name, Description, and Gender Restriction**  

‚ùπ **Click Next to choose multiple Services to include in the package**  

‚ù∫ **Continue to set Pricing: Walk-in, Online, and Off-Peak rates**  

‚ùª **Select the Venues where the package is available**  

‚ùº **Assign Staff Members to deliver the package services**  

‚ùΩ **Click the "Save" button to finalize**  

‚ùæ **A confirmation message confirms package creation**  

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Ensure your services are pre-configured so you can add them smoothly to your package.
