## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Access the Product Menu**  
Inside the **Product Catalog**, click the **three-dot menu** next to a product or open the product details and use the menu there.

❷ **Click Edit**  
Click **Edit**.

❸ **Update the Fields**  
Update fields across **Basic Info**, **Categorization**, **Pricing**, or **Stock**.

❹ **Save Changes**  
Click **Save** to update changes.

❺ **Confirmation Message**  
A confirmation message will appear.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Editing helps you keep information current without recreating a product.
