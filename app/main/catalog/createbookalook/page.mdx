## Create a Book a Look

 

*Purpose*: Add new fashion looks to your catalog, complete with pricing, venue availability, and staff assignments.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **From the main menu, click on "Catalog" on the left**  

‚ù∑ **Switch to the "Book a Look" tab in the catalog navigation bar**  

‚ù∏ **Click the "Add Book a Look" button on the top-right**  

‚ùπ **A form panel will open to collect necessary information**:  
- Upload the **photo**, enter the **Look Name**, **Description**, **Gender Restriction**, **Duration**, and **Category**.

‚ù∫ **Click Next to proceed to the Pricing Section**, where you can set **Walk-in**, **Off-Peak**, and **Online** prices.  
- You can toggle between booking methods: **Online**, **Walk-In**, or **Both**.

‚ùª **In the following steps, select the venues where the look is offered and assign the staff members responsible**

‚ùº **Click Save to create the new Look**

‚ùΩ **A success message will confirm the Look was created**

---

**Tip**: Ensure all required fields are filled out before saving for a smooth creation experience.
