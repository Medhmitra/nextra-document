## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **Go to the Book a Look section under the Catalog**  

‚ù∑ **To edit**, either click on the **three-dot menu** next to the Look and choose **Edit**, or open the detail panel by clicking the Look itself and access the **Edit** option from the top three-dot menu.  

‚ù∏ **Once the update panel opens**, you can revise the **Basic Info**, **Photo**, **Description**, **Pricing**, **Venue**, **Staff**, and **Booking Methods**.  

‚ùπ **Click the "Save" button to apply changes**  

‚ù∫ **A confirmation message will appear once saved**

---

**Tip**: Double-check all your edits before saving to avoid incomplete updates.
