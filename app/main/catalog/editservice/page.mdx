## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Navigate to the Catalog > Services section**  
From the left-hand side menu on the main dashboard.

‚ù∑ **Locate the service you wish to update**  
Click on the **three-dot menu** in the respective row and select **"Edit"**.  
Alternatively, click the **Service Name** to open the detail panel and use the top-right **three-dot menu** ‚Üí **"Edit"**.

‚ù∏ **Make changes in the editable panel**  
Update any section like **Basic Information**, **Pricing**, **Venue**, or **Staff assignments**.

‚ùπ **Click the "Save" button**  
Your updates will be applied.

‚ù∫ **Confirmation Message**  
You‚Äôll see a confirmation that the service has been successfully updated.
