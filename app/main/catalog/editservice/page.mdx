## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Navigate to the Catalog > Services section**  
From the left-hand side menu on the main dashboard.

❷ **Locate the service you wish to update**  
Click on the **three-dot menu** in the respective row and select **"Edit"**.  
Alternatively, click the **Service Name** to open the detail panel and use the top-right **three-dot menu** → **"Edit"**.

❸ **Make changes in the editable panel**  
Update any section like **Basic Information**, **Pricing**, **Venue**, or **Staff assignments**.

❹ **Click the "Save" button**  
Your updates will be applied.

❺ **Confirmation Message**  
You’ll see a confirmation that the service has been successfully updated.

---

**Tip for Efficient Edits**:  
Plan updates during low-traffic times to avoid interruptions for clients or staff.
