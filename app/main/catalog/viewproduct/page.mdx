*Purpose*: See complete product details including sales activity, stock levels, and categorization.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Click on a Product**  
Click on a product name from the list.

‚ù∑ **View the Details Panel**  
A details panel will show:
- **General Info**, **Categorization**, **Pricing**, **Venue-wise Stock**, and **Sales Activity** (purchase history, payment status).

‚ù∏ **Check Alerts**  
Also displays **Low Stock Alerts** and **Post-sale changes**.

‚ùπ **Close the Panel**  
Click **Close** to return.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Product detail panels provide a quick overview for support, stock checks, or pricing reviews.
