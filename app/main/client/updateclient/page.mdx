## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

➊ **From the Clients page**, locate the client whose information you want to update.

➋ On the right end of that client’s row, click the **three-dot (⋮) menu**.

➌ From the dropdown, select **“Edit”**.

➍ A panel will open where you can modify details such as:
- **Address**
- **Partner Notes**

➎ After making the desired changes, click the **“Update Client”** button at the bottom.

➏ A confirmation message will notify you that the update was successful.

➐ If you wish to cancel your changes, click the **“Cancel”** button at the top of the panel.
