*Purpose*: Add a new client to the system to manage their appointments and records.

## Steps:



❶ **Go to Clients Page**  
From the dashboard, go to the **“Clients”** tab on the left.

❷ **Click Add Client**  
On the top right corner of the Clients page, click the **“Add Client”** button.

❸ **Open the Form**  
A slide-in panel will open on the right side of your screen with a form to fill in client information.

❹ **Verify Phone Number**  
Begin by entering the client’s **phone number**, then click the **“Check”** button to verify if the number is unique (no duplicates allowed).

❺ **Fill Out Form Fields**  
Once verified, continue filling out the rest of the form fields:
- **Full Name**
- **Email**
- **Gender**
- **Birth Date**
- **Address**
- Optionally upload a **profile image (avatar)**

❻ **Add Notes**  
You can also add a **partner note**, which can be internal preferences or instructions.

❼ **Submit the Form**  
After all required fields are completed, scroll to the bottom of the panel and click on the **“Add Client”** button.

❽ **Confirmation Message**  
A success message will appear confirming that the new client has been added to your system.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Ensure all mandatory fields are filled correctly to avoid validation errors during submission.
