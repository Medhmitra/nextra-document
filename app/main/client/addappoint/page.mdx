##  Add Appointment from Client Page

 

*Purpose*: Quickly create a new appointment directly from an individual client’s profile page.

## Steps:



❶ **Go to the Clients Page**  
From the dashboard, navigate to the **Clients** page.

❷ **Locate the Client**  
Find the client you want to book for, then click the **three-dot menu** on their row.

❸ **Select “Add Appointment”**  
Choose **“Add Appointment”** from the dropdown menu.

❹ **Fill in Appointment Details**  
The **Appointment Creation Panel** will appear on the right. Enter:
- **Venue**  
- **Date and Time**  
- **Services**  
- **Assigned Staff**  
- **Status** (New, Confirmed, etc.)

❺ **Create the Appointment**  
Click **“Create Appointment”** once all details are completed.

❻ **Confirmation**  
A success message will appear, and the client’s appointment count will be updated.

---

**Tip for Staff**:  
This method is especially useful when handling a client query over phone or chat and booking in real time.
