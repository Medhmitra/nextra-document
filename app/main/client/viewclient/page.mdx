## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **Navigate to the Clients Page**  
From the left-side menu, click on **Clients**.

‚ù∑ **Click on the Client's Name**  
Locate the client you're interested in and click directly on their **name**.

‚ù∏ **View the Profile Panel**  
A detailed profile panel will slide in from the right side.

‚ùπ **Review Client Information**  
The panel includes complete contact and activity info:
- **Phone number, email, gender, address**
- **Birth date, anniversary (if added), and date created**
- **Total number of appointments**
- **History of sales, vouchers, memberships, gift cards, and product purchases**

‚ù∫ **Close the Panel**  
Click the **X (Close)** icon on the top right corner to close the profile.

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
This feature helps teams provide personalized service by understanding client history and preferences.
