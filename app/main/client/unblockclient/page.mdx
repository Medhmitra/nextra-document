## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Go to the Clients Page**  
From the **Clients** page, find the blocked client in the list (status will show as “Blocked”).

❷ **Open the Options Menu**  
Click on the **three-dot menu** at the end of their row.

❸ **Choose "Unblock"**  
Select the **“Unblock”** option.

❹ **Client is Reactivated**  
The client will immediately be reactivated.

❺ **Confirmation Message**  
A message will confirm that the client is now **active again** and can once again make appointments or purchases.

---

**Tip for Admins or Managers**:  
Unblocking restores full access for the client, including booking and purchasing capabilities.
