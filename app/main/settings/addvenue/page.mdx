*Purpose*: Learn how to create a new venue with complete setup of address, amenities, billing, and more.

## Steps:



❶ **From the main dashboard, click on the Settings option located on the left sidebar.**  
❷ **Under the Venue Management section, click on the Venue card (first navigation tab).**  
❸ **Inside the venue section, click the “Add Venue” button at the top-right corner.**  
❹ **A multi-step panel will open:**
- **Step 1: Basic Info** – Upload up to 5 venue images, fill out the venue name, slug, franchise details, gender restriction, phone number, email, and business type.  
- **Step 2: Address** – Enter the complete address details of the venue.  
- **Step 3: Amenities** – Select applicable amenities such as WiFi, lotion, private rooms, etc.  
- **Step 4: Operating Hours** – Set working days, opening and closing hours, and define any off-peak timings.  
- **Step 5: Billing Info** – Enter billing name, venue name, billing address.  
- **Step 6: Workstations** – Add workstation names for staff or service areas.  
❺ **After completing all fields, click Save to successfully add the venue.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Setting up venues properly helps streamline operations, assign staff, and organize service locations efficiently.
