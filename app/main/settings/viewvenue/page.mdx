## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **From the left sidebar, click Settings, then navigate to the Venue section.**  
‚ù∑ **In the venue list, click on the venue name you wish to view.**  
‚ù∏ **You'll be directed to the venue's detailed information page:**
- Shows venue status (e.g., hidden or visible to clients).  
- Displays all information including name, franchise, contact details, opening hours, amenities.  
- A warning box may appear if the venue is not subscribed.
‚ùπ **To return, click the Back button to go back to the venue listing.**
