## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Navigate to Settings > Venue.**  
❷ **Click on the venue name or image you want to edit.**  
❸ **In the top section of the venue detail page, click the Edit icon.**  
❹ **A panel will open where you can update any section:**
- Basic info  
- Address  
- Amenities  
- Operating hours  
- Billing details  
- Workstations  
❺ **Once edits are done, click the Save button to apply the changes.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Always review the changes before saving to avoid overwriting important venue details.
