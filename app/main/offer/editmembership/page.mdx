## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Navigate to Offers > Membership**  
Navigate to **Offers > Membership**.

❷ **Open the Edit Menu**  
Click the **3-dot menu** on the desired membership row and select **Edit**.  
- Or, click the **Membership Name** and choose **Edit** from the top-right 3-dot menu.

❸ **Update Membership Details**  
Update fields such as **Basic Info**, **Pricing**, or **Venue**.

❹ **Save Your Changes**  
Click **Save** to confirm changes.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Make sure all updates reflect your current offers and venue availability before saving.
