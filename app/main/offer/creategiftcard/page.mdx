## Create Gift Card

  

*Purpose*: Create and configure a gift card for online or walk-in sales in the Offers section.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Navigate to the Offers Section**  
Go to the **Offers** section.

‚ù∑ **Open the Gift Card Tab**  
Click the **Gift Card** tab.

‚ù∏ **Click Add Gift Card**  
Click the **Add Gift Card** button at the top-right.

‚ùπ **Fill in the Gift Card Details**  
Complete the form with:
- **Card Template**, **Name**, **Validity**, and **Description**.
- Click **Next** to configure **Pricing**:
  - Enable **Online** and/or **Walk-in** sales.
  - Enter **Total Value** and **Discount Percentage**.
- Click **Next** to assign **Venue(s)**.

‚ù∫ **Save the Gift Card**  
Click **Save** to create the gift card.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Ensure the venues and pricing settings are accurate before saving.
