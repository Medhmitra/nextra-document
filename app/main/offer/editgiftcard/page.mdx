*Purpose*: Edit the details of an existing gift card including basic information, pricing, or assigned venue.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Go to the Gift Card Section**  
Navigate to **Offers > Gift Card**.

‚ù∑ **Access the Edit Option**  
Click the **3-dot menu** on the gift card row and select **Edit**.

‚ù∏ **Make Necessary Changes**  
Update required fields: **Basic Info**, **Pricing**, or **Venue**.

‚ùπ **Save the Changes**  
Click **Save** to apply changes.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Review all fields carefully before saving to avoid missing any important details.
