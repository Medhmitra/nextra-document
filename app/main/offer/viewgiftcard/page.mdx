## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Navigate to Gift Card Section**  
Go to **Offers > Gift Card**.

‚ù∑ **Open Gift Card Details**  
Click on the **Gift Card Name** to open the full details panel.

‚ù∏ **Review Gift Card Information**  
Inside the panel, review:
- **Basic Info**
- **Pricing**
- **Venue**
- **Client Usage**

‚ùπ **Exit the Panel**  
Click the **Cancel icon** at the top-right to return to the main page.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
This is useful when confirming settings before promotions or client purchases.
