## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Go to Gift Card Section**  
Navigate to **Offers > Gift Card**.

‚ù∑ **Open Delete Option**  
Click the **3-dot menu** next to the gift card and choose **Delete**.

‚ù∏ **Confirm Deletion**  
In the confirmation popup, click **Delete** to permanently remove the card.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Be cautious‚Äîonce deleted, the gift card cannot be restored.
