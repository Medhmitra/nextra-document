## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **Go to Offers > Voucher**  
Navigate to the **Offers** section in the left menu, then click on **Voucher**.

‚ù∑ **Click the 3-dot menu**  
On the voucher row, click the **3-dot menu** and choose **Delete**.  
- Alternatively, click the **Voucher Name** and use the **3-dot menu** from the details panel.

‚ù∏ **Confirm Deletion**  
Approve the deletion in the confirmation pop-up that appears.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Once deleted, the voucher cannot be recovered. Make sure to confirm before proceeding.
