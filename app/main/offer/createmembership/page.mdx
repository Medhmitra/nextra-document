## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **Navigate to Offers**  
Go to the **Offers** section.

‚ù∑ **Click Membership Tab**  
Select the **Membership** tab from the top.

‚ù∏ **Add a New Membership**  
Click the **Add Membership** button to begin.

‚ùπ **Fill Out the Form**  
Complete the form fields:
- Choose a **Card Template**
- Enter a **Name**, **Validity**, and **Description**
- Click **Next**

‚ù∫ **Configure Pricing**  
Enable sale types and input values:
- Toggle **Online** or **Walk-in** sale
- Enter **Total Value** and **Membership Price**
- Click **Next**

‚ùª **Select Venue(s)**  
Choose one or more venues where this membership will be available.

‚ùº **Save the Membership**  
Click the **Save** button to finalize and create the membership.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Ensure you‚Äôve double-checked pricing and venue selections before saving.
