*Purpose*: Quickly view key details of any membership plan within the Offers section.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Go to Offers > Membership**  
Navigate to the **Offers** section, then click on **Membership**.

‚ù∑ **Click the Membership Name**  
Find the membership you want and click on its **name** to open the detail page.

‚ù∏ **Review the Sections**  
View all the relevant details including:
- **Basic Info**
- **Pricing**
- **Venue**
- **Client Activity**

‚ùπ **Exit the Details Page**  
Click the **Cancel** icon (top left or top right depending on your layout) to return to the membership list.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Use this view to double-check all your setup before launching offers or sharing with clients.
