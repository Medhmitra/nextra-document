*Purpose*: Create promotional vouchers with service inclusions, pricing, and availability.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Navigate to Offers**  
From the left menu, go to the **Offers** section.

‚ù∑ **Go to Voucher Tab**  
Click on the **Voucher** tab at the top.

‚ù∏ **Click Add Voucher**  
Hit the **Add Voucher** button located at the top-right corner.

‚ùπ **Fill in the Voucher Form**  
A panel opens with fields to complete:
- **Voucher Name**, **Gender Restriction**, **Validity**, **Total Sessions**, and **Description**.
- Click **Next** to go to **Service Inclusion** and pick the services to include.
- Click **Next** again to reach **Service Pricing**:
  - Enable **Online Booking** or **Walk-in Booking** as needed.
  - Enter **Total Service Value** and **Voucher Price**.
- Click **Next** one more time to choose **Venue(s)** where this voucher is valid.

‚ù∫ **Save the Voucher**  
Click **Save** to complete voucher creation.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Be sure to define relevant services and pricing for maximum value.
