## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Go to Offers > Voucher**  
Navigate to the **Offers** section in the left menu, then click on **Voucher**.

❷ **Click on the Voucher Name**  
This will open the voucher's details panel.

❸ **View the Following Details**  
- **Basic Info**
- **Service List**
- **Venue(s)**
- **Sales Activity**
- **Client Usage**

❹ **Exit the Panel**  
Click the **Cancel** icon to close the details panel.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Useful for reviewing voucher engagement, usage patterns, and assigned services/venues.
