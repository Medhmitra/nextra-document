## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **Navigate to Offers > Membership**  
Open the **Offers** section and click on **Membership**.

‚ù∑ **Click the 3-dot menu**  
Next to the membership you wish to delete, click the **3-dot menu** and select **Delete**.  
- You can also find this option from the membership details view.

‚ù∏ **Confirm the deletion**  
A confirmation dialog will appear. Confirm the action to permanently remove the membership.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Be cautious before deleting, as this action cannot be undone.
