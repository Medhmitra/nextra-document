## Filter by Venue, Staff, or Status

*Purpose*: Easily find specific appointments and manage your schedule with precision.

### Steps:

## Filtering by Venue


*Use this when you want to see appointments for a specific salon branch.*

### Steps:

‚ù∂ **Navigate to Calendar**
    
    From the dashboard, click on the  **Calendar** option in the left sidebar.
    
‚ù∑ **Access the Filter Options**
    
    Once on the Calendar page, look at the top navigation bar near the "Calendar" heading.
    
    You‚Äôll see three filter dropdowns: **Venue**, **Staff**, and **Status**. Click the **first dropdown** labeled **Venue**.
    
‚ù∏ **Choose a Venue**
    
    A dropdown list of all available salon venues will appear.
    
    Select the venue you want to view.
    
‚ùπ **View Results**
    
    The calendar now refreshes to show only **appointments scheduled at the selected venue**.
    
    This helps you focus on a single location's activities.
    
‚ù∫ **Clear the Filter**
    
    To reset the view, simply click the **Venue dropdown again** and either:
    
    - Click the same venue again to deselect, or
    - Select "All Venues" or leave the field empty (depending on system behavior).

## Filtering by Staff


*Use this when you want to focus on one team member‚Äôs appointments.*

### Steps:

‚ù∂ **Open the Calendar**
    
    From the main page, click **Calendar** in the left sidebar.
    
‚ù∑ **Select the Staff Filter**
    
    On the top navigation of the Calendar page, click the **second dropdown**, which is labeled **Staff**.
    
‚ù∏ **Choose a Staff Member**
    
    A dropdown list will appear showing all active staff.
    
    Click the name of the team member whose schedule you want to check.
    
‚ùπ **View Results**
    
    The calendar will now show **only appointments assigned to that staff member**.
    
‚ù∫ **Clear the Filter**
    
    To remove the filter, click the **Staff dropdown** again and reset it to show all staff schedules.
    

---

## Filtering by Appointment Status


*Use this to filter by appointment state like Confirmed, Cancelled, etc.*

### Steps:

‚ù∂ **Go to Calendar**
    
    Open the **Calendar** page from the left navigation menu.
    
‚ù∑ **Click on the Status Dropdown**
    
    On the top right, next to Venue and Staff filters, you‚Äôll see the **third dropdown** ‚Äì **Status**.
    
‚ù∏ **Select a Status**
    
    Available status options include:
    
    - **New** ‚Äì Recently created appointments awaiting confirmation
    - **Confirmed** ‚Äì Approved appointments scheduled by the salon
    - **Completed** ‚Äì Services already completed
    - **Cancelled** ‚Äì Appointments canceled by the salon or client
    - **No Show** ‚Äì Appointments missed by the client without cancellation
‚ùπ **View the Filtered Results**
    
    The calendar will update to display appointments **matching only the selected status**, giving you a clear snapshot.
    
‚ù∫ **Clear the Filter**
    
    Click the **Status dropdown again** and reset it by deselecting or choosing "All Statuses".
