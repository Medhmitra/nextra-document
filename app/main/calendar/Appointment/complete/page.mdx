## Mark a client appointment as complete

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Mark an appointment as completed</h2>

❶ Navigate to the [**Appointments**](https://partner.dasalon.com/dashboard/appointments) section from the left-hand menu.

❷ In the appointment list:
   - Click **⋮ > Edit** and change status to **Completed**  
   - OR open the appointment detail panel → change status to **Completed**  
   - OR use the status dropdown in the row and select **Completed**  
   - OR click **Checkout** after finishing the service

❸ Click **Update/Save** if using the edit form.

❹ The status of the appointment will now appear in **green**.

---

**Important**: A completed appointment updates reports, dashboards, and ensures staff payout is correctly recorded.
