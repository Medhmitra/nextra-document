## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step</h2>

‚ù∂ **Go to Calendar**  
From the main sidebar, click on **Calendar**.

‚ù∑ **Locate the View Toggle**  
In the **top-right corner** of the calendar, you'll see two options:  
- **Day View**  
- **Week View**

‚ù∏ **View by Day (Default)**  
Click **Day** to view:  
- Staff timelines by the hour  
- Time gaps to add more bookings  
- Real-time visibility into each slot

‚ùπ **View by Week**  
Click **Week** to get:  
- A full week overview  
- How appointments are distributed  
- Workload comparison across staff

‚ù∫ **Switch Anytime**  
Toggle between the two as needed based on planning or operations.

---

**Tip**:  
Use **Day View** for deep planning or when managing walk-ins.  
Use **Week View** to spot trends, balance workloads, or fill underbooked days.
