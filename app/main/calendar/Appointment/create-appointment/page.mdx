## Create an appointment for a specific time

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Create an appointment for a specific time</h2>

‚ù∂ From the main page, look at the left-hand menu and click on  
   [**Appointments**](https://partner.dasalon.com/dashboard/appointments) to open the appointments section.

‚ù∑ On the Appointments page, locate the **‚ÄúAdd Appointment‚Äù** button in the top right corner.  
   Click this [**"Add Appointments"**](https://partner.dasalon.com/dashboard/appointments?sheet=add-appointment) to open the appointment creation panel.

‚ù∏ In the appointment creation panel, click **Customer** to:
   - Select an existing client
   - Create a new client
   - Leave it as a walk-in appointment

   <img src="/assets/apppanel.png" alt="Appointment Panel" style={{ width: '60%', maxWidth: '600px' }} />

‚ùπ Click on **Venue** to select the location where the service will be provided.

‚ù∫ Select a service from the list to add it to the appointment:

   - Select one or more services offered at the selected venue  
   - To customize service details, simply click on the edit option in the corner of the selected service.

   <img src="/assets/appservice.png" alt="Service Selection" style={{ width: '60%', maxWidth: '600px' }} />

‚ùª Once all details are filled in, click the **Create Appointment** button at the bottom of the panel.

   <img src="/assets/appcreate.png" alt="Create Appointment Button" style={{ width: '60%', maxWidth: '600px' }} />

‚ùº Your new appointment will now appear in the calendar view, assigned to the selected staff and time.
