##  **Delete Appointment**

*Purpose*: Permanently remove the appointment. Only allowed if the appointment is in **New**, **Confirmed**, or **No-Show** status.

### Steps:

❶ Go to **Appointments**.  
❷ For eligible statuses, Row menu → click **⋮ → Delete** OR open the detail panel → **⋮ → Delete**  
❸ **Completed** and **Cancelled** appointments **cannot** be deleted.  
❹ A confirmation popup appears.  
❺ Click **Confirm Delete**, a success message will confirm the deletion.  
❻ Deleted records will no longer show in reports or history.
