## **No-Show Appointment**

*Purpose*: Record when a client doesn’t show up. Important for tracking client reliability and blocking last-minute gaps.

### Steps:

❶ Open **Appointments**.
❷ Click **⋮ → Edit** or From status dropdown  
   - Edit panel → change status  
   - Appointment ID panel  
❸ Select **No-Show**.  
❹ Click **Update**.  
❺ Appointment turns **Grey**
