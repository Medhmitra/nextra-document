### Reschedule Appointment

<p className="mb-4"><strong>Purpose</strong>: Change the date/time of an appointment due to client or staff needs.</p>

### <h3 className="text-xl font-medium text-gray-800 mb-2"> Steps: </h3>

<p className="mb-2">❶ Go to the <Link href="https://partner.dasalon.com/dashboard/appointments" className="text-blue-600 underline font-medium">Appointments</Link> section from the sidebar.</p>

<p className="mb-2">❷ Click <strong>⋮ → Reschedule</strong> from the appointment row</p>

<p className="mb-2">OR</p>

<ul className="mb-2 list-disc list-inside">
  <li>Click on the <strong>Appointment ID</strong></li>
  <li>Then click <strong>⋮ → Reschedule</strong> from the side panel.</li>
</ul>

<p className="mb-2">❸ A reschedule prompt will appear.</p>
<p className="mb-2">❹ Select a <strong>new date and/or time</strong>.</p>
<p className="mb-2">❺ Click <strong>Reschedule</strong> to save.</p>

<p className="mb-4">✅ A confirmation message shows the appointment has been rescheduled.</p>

<p className="mb-6"><strong>Tip</strong>: Avoid last-minute reschedules if your salon has a cancellation/rescheduling policy.</p>
