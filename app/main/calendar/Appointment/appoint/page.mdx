*Purpose*: Manage all aspects of an appointment ‚Äî from editing and rescheduling to canceling, deleting, and checking out.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Go to Calendar**  
From the left sidebar, click **Calendar**.

‚ù∑ **Open Appointment Details**  
In the calendar grid, find and click on any **booked appointment**.  
A **detailed panel** will slide open on the right.

‚ù∏ **Update or Edit Appointment**  
- Click the **three-dot menu** (‚ãÆ) at the top-right of the detail panel  
- Choose **Edit**  
- Modify client, service, date/time, or assigned staff  
- Save the updated details

‚ùπ **Reschedule the Appointment**  
- Either:  
  - Click **Reschedule** at the bottom of the panel, or  
  - Use the **three-dot menu > Reschedule**  
- Select new date/time and confirm

‚ù∫ **Cancel the Appointment**  
- Click the **three-dot menu > Cancel**  
- Choose or enter a **reason for cancellation**

‚ùª **Delete the Appointment** *(if allowed)*  
- Click **three-dot menu > Delete**  
- Confirm deletion when prompted

‚ùº **Change Appointment Status**  
- Locate the **status button** at the top-left of the panel  
- Click and change to:  
  - **New**  
  - **Confirmed**  
  - **Completed**  
  - **No-Show**  
  - **Cancelled**

‚ùΩ **Checkout the Appointment**  
- Scroll down and click **Checkout**  
- This redirects you to the **Sales Page** where payment is processed

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
You can manage everything without leaving the calendar ‚Äî saves time and helps avoid missing details.
