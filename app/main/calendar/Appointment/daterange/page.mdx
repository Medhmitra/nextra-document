‚ù∑ **Find the Date Controls**  
At the top-left of the calendar page, **below the filters**, you‚Äôll see three date control buttons:

‚ù∏ **View Previous Appointments**

‚ùπ **View Upcoming Appointments**

‚ù∫ **Jump to Today‚Äôs Appointments**
