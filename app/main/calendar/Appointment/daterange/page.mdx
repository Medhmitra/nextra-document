## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Open the Calendar**  
On the main dashboard, click on **Calendar** in the left-side menu.

❷ **Find the Date Controls**  
At the top-left of the calendar page, **below the filters**, you’ll see three date control buttons:
- **Previous**
- **Today**
- **Next**

❸ **View Previous Appointments**  
Click the **Previous** button to scroll back and view appointments from earlier days.

❹ **View Upcoming Appointments**  
Click the **Next** button to scroll ahead and see what’s coming up in the following days.

❺ **Jump to Today’s Appointments**  
The **Today** button is highlighted by default.  
If you’ve scrolled away, click it anytime to come back to today’s bookings.

❻ **Pick a Custom Date Range**  
In the **center-top of the calendar**, click on the displayed date (e.g., “15 Jun 2025”)  
A date picker opens. You can now select:
- A **start date** and an **end date**
- The calendar will refresh and display **appointments within that range**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
This is especially helpful when you're reviewing **past performance**, tracking **busy periods**, or checking **future availability**.
