## Cancel a client appointment

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Create an appointment for a specific time</h2>

❶ Navigate to the [**Appointments**](https://partner.dasalon.com/dashboard/appointments) section from the left-hand menu.

❷ In the appointment list:
   - Click **⋮ > Cancel** from the desired appointment row  
   - OR open the appointment detail panel → click **⋮ > Cancel**  
   - OR click **Edit** and change the status to **"Cancelled"**

❸ A prompt will appear asking for a **reason**.  
   - Choose from pre-filled suggestions like “Unwell” or “Emergency”  
   - Or write your own custom reason

❹ Click **Cancel Appointment** to confirm.

❺ The status of the appointment will now appear in **red**.

---

**Important**: A cancelled appointment is never deleted. It stays in the system for analytics, reporting, and audit purposes.
