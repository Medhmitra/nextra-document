*Purpose*: For card transactions via POS (debit/credit).

## Steps:



❶ **Click on “Card” payment method.**  
❷ **Enter the amount paid by card.**  
❸ **Click Add Transaction.**

Useful when:

- Accepting payments via swipe machines.  
- Client prefers secure non-cash options.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Use this method for fast, secure transactions without handling physical cash.
