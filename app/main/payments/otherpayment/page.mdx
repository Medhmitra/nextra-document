## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Choose “Other”**  
Select “Other” as the payment method.

❷ **Enter the Custom Amount**  
Fill in the amount and optionally include a note (e.g., voucher code).

❸ **Click Add Transaction**  
Add the transaction to finalize the record.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Useful when accepting daSalon gift cards, compensating with promo credits, or making manual adjustments.
