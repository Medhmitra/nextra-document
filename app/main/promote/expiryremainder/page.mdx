## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Access the “Promote” section from the sidebar.**

❷ **Go to the “Message Center.”**

❸ **Click on the “Expiry Reminders – Client” option.**

❹ **This shows alerts configured to notify clients about upcoming expiry of:**
- Gift cards
- Vouchers
- Memberships

❺ **Enable or disable based on preference, and use the template view to check or modify the message format.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Setting up expiry reminders can help drive renewals and reduce customer churn.
