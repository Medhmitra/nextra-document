## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Click on “Promote” from the main navigation panel.**

❷ **Navigate to the “Message Center” tab.**

❸ **Select “Sales Update – Client.”**

❹ **This section covers automated notifications for purchase confirmations.**

❺ **Toggle ON/OFF as required and view templates as needed.**
