## Sales update - Client in Message Center

  

*Purpose*: Notify clients when they purchase an item like a voucher, gift card, or membership.

## Steps:



❶ **Click on “Promote” from the main navigation panel.**

❷ **Navigate to the “Message Center” tab.**

❸ **Select “Sales Update – Client.”**

❹ **This section covers automated notifications for purchase confirmations.**

❺ **Toggle ON/OFF as required and view templates as needed.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Ensuring timely confirmation messages can improve customer satisfaction and reduce support inquiries.
