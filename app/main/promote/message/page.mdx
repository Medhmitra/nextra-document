## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Navigate to the “Promote” section from the dashboard.**

❷ **In the “Message Center” tab, locate and click on “Campaign Messages.”**

❸ **To start a new campaign:**
- Click on the **“New Campaign”** button.
- Choose the category you wish to promote (e.g., Service, Package, Voucher, Membership, Gift Card, or Promo Code).
- Select or upload a visual template (image or poster).
- Define your **target audience** based on criteria like age, gender, or client status.

❹ **Schedule the campaign and send the message via SMS or other enabled channels.**

❺ **Manage existing campaigns, enable/disable messages, and preview campaign performance.**
