## Steps:



‚ù∂ **Go to the Promote Section**  
From the main dashboard, click on the **"Promote"** section on the left-hand navigation menu.

‚ù∑ **Access the Get Featured Tab**  
In the Promote module, go to the **"Get Featured"** tab (the first tab).

‚ù∏ **Click Add Featured**  
Click on the **"Add Featured"** button located at the top right corner of the page.

‚ùπ **Fill the Required Details**  
A side panel will open. Fill out the required details:
- Select the **venue** you want to promote.
- Choose the **duration** (1 week, 2 weeks, or 4 weeks).
- Pick the **start date** and **end date**. The cost will be calculated at ‚Çπ500 per week.
- If available, enter a **promo code** to apply a discount.

‚ù∫ **Make the Payment**  
Make the payment using the **daSalon Wallet** by clicking the **"Pay"** button.

‚ùª **Submit for Review**  
Once paid, your Get Featured request will be submitted for review. This is a smart strategy to increase visibility during new openings or low booking periods.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Use this during salon launches or to boost traffic during off-peak periods.
