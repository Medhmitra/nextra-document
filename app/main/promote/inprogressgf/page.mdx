## Steps:



❶ **Live Campaign**  
When the featured listing is currently live (between start and end dates), the status changes to **"In Progress"**.

❷ **Go to Get Featured**  
Visit the **Get Featured** tab to view it.

❸ **Filter Listings**  
Use the **Status Filter** to view only **"In Progress"** campaigns.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
"In Progress" means your promotion is active right now. Keep an eye on performance and engagement.
