*Purpose*: Learn how to track and view completed Get Featured submissions.

## Steps:



❶ **Go to the Get Featured Section**  
Navigate to the **Get Featured** section under the **Promote** page.

❷ **Status Automatically Updates**  
Once your selected start and end dates pass, and the listing duration completes, the system automatically updates the status to **"Completed"**.

❸ **Filter by Completed**  
To view only completed entries, apply the **Status Filter** and choose **"Completed"**.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Tracking completed listings helps in reviewing past promotions and planning future ones.
