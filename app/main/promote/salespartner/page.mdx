## Sale update -Partner in Message Center

 

*Purpose*: Notify salon staff when a client purchases a gift card, voucher, or membership.

## Steps:



❶ **From the main navigation, go to the “Promote” menu.**

❷ **Click into the “Message Center.”**

❸ **Choose the option labeled “Sales Update – Partner.”**

❹ **The panel will display triggers for purchases like gift cards, vouchers, or memberships.**

❺ **Enable or disable automated messages based on your need.**

❻ **Click to view the pre-configured templates.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Sales notifications help the team stay informed about client engagement and revenue boosts.
