## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

‚ù∂ **Navigate to the Ad Manager**  
Go to the **Promote** section and select the **Ad Manager** tab.

‚ù∑ **Click Add Ad**  
Press **Add Ad** to open the ad creation panel.

‚ù∏ **Fill in Required Details**  
- Choose platform: **Facebook**, **Instagram**  
- Select type: **Image** or **Video**  
- Upload creatives, set campaign name, description, goal (e.g., promote coupon)  
- Set target audience: age, gender, location  
- Choose schedule, set budget  
- Proceed to payment via card

‚ùπ **Launch the Ad**  
Click **"Pay Now"** to publish and launch the advertisement.

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Plan your ad content and creatives in advance to speed up the setup and ensure better results.
