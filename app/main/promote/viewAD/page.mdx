## View Ad in Ad Manager



*Purpose*: See all the important details about any ad you've created or launched through the platform.

## Steps:



‚ù∂ **In the Ad Manager tab, click on the Ad Name to view details**

‚ù∑ **You can view:**
- Campaign name, platform, type
- Start/end time, budget
- Visual preview
- Performance stats: clicks, shares, impressions, etc.

‚ù∏ **Use the Cancel (X) button to close the panel**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
You can easily track the effectiveness of each campaign and decide which strategies work best.
