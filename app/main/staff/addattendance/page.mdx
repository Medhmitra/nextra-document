## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **On the dashboard, navigate to the “Staff” section.**  
❷ **Click on the “Attendance” tab from the top navigation menu.**  
❸ **Click the “Add Attendance” button at the top-right corner.**  
❹ **A right-side panel will open where you must choose the Attendance Type: Present, Absent, or Half Day.**  
❺ **Select the Staff Name, then choose the Date for the attendance entry.**  
❻ **Enter Clock-in and Clock-out times, and optionally add Remarks.**  
❼ **Click “Add Attendance” to save the entry.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Helps in tracking punctuality, managing payroll, and monitoring shift hours accurately.
