## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Navigate to the “Staff” section from the left-hand menu of the main dashboard.**  
❷ **To edit staff details, click on the “three-dot menu” at the end of any staff member’s row and select “Edit”.**  
❸ **Alternatively, click on the staff member’s name to open their profile, then click the “three-dot menu” in the top-right and choose “Edit”.**  
❹ **An editable panel will appear where you can modify the basic information, commission structure, or assigned venues.**  
❺ **After making the necessary changes, click “Save” to update the staff member’s details.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Use the individual staff panel for deeper customization or when making major role changes.
