## Add Leave



*Purpose*: Allow managers to log and approve staff leave days with optional remarks and tracking.

## Steps:



❶ **Navigate to the Staff section in the main dashboard.**  
❷ **Click on the “Leave” tab located in the top navigation.**  
❸ **In the Leave page, click the “Add Leave” button on the top-right.**  
❹ **A side panel will appear. Check the box for “Leave Approved” if applicable.**  
❺ **Select the Leave Type (Planned or Taken).**  
❻ **Choose the Staff Name from the dropdown.**  
❼ **Enter the Start Date and End Date of the leave.**  
❽ **Optionally add Remarks.**  
❾ **Click “Add Leave” to save the entry.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Use remarks to note reasons or special cases—makes tracking and approvals easier.
