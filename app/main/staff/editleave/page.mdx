## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Inside the Leave tab under the Staff section, locate the leave record you want to edit.**  
❷ **Click on the three-dot menu next to the leave record and select “Edit”.**  
❸ **A panel will open where you can modify the Leave Type, Staff Name, Dates, and Remarks.**  
❹ **Click on “Save” to update the leave record.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Use remarks to document changes—this helps in maintaining clear history and communication.
