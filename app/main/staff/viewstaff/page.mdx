## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **Go to the “Staff” section from the main dashboard.**  
❷ **To view a staff member’s full profile, click on their name directly in the list.**  
❸ **Alternatively, use the “three-dot menu” next to the staff member’s row and choose “View”.**  
❹ **A detailed profile panel will open displaying information such as contact details, job role, commissions, and assigned venues.**  
❺ **Click the cancel/close icon at the top of the panel to exit the view.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Use this to quickly verify staff roles or commission setups before making edits or scheduling shifts.
