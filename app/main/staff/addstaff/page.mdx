## Steps:

<h2 className="text-2xl font-semibold text-gray-800 mt-8 mb-4">Step-by-step Instructions</h2>

❶ **On the main dashboard, click on the “Staff” option located in the left-hand menu.**  
❷ **Inside the Staff section, ensure you are in the “Members” tab (default tab).**  
❸ **Click on the “Add Staff” button found in the top-right corner of the screen.**  
❹ **A side panel will appear with a form. Begin by uploading an image for the staff member.**  
❺ **Fill in all required basic information such as name, email, phone number, gender, job role, and specialization.**  
❻ **Provide links to their Facebook and Instagram profiles if available.**  
❼ **Add the address of the staff member.**  
❽ **Click Next to proceed to the commission section.**  
❾ **Set commission percentages for different categories: Service, Book-a-Look, Package, Voucher, Membership, and Product.**  
❿ **Click Next to assign one or more venues where this staff member will work.**  
⓫ **After reviewing all the details, click on the “Save” button to create the staff profile.**

---

**Tip for First-Time Users**:  
Be sure to assign venues and commissions correctly to avoid staff visibility or payout issues.
